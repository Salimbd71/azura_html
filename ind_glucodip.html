Glucodip MXR

Therapeutic Class:

Anti-diabetic (DPP-4 inhibitors & Biguanides)

Indication:

  • Sitagliptin indicated as an adjunct to diet and exercise to improve glycemic control in adults with type 2 diabetes mellitus।
  • Combination product indicated as an adjunct to diet and exercise to improve glycemic control in adults with type 2 diabetes mellitus when treatment with both Sitagliptin and Metformin is appropriate।

Positioning:

Glucodip MXR: In T2DM patients uncontrolled on SU + Met.
