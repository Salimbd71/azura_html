Approved Indications

  • Acute coronary syndrome (unstable angina / myocardial infarction)
  • Recent MI, recent stroke, or established peripheral arterial disease
  • Shown to reduce the rate of MI and stroke

Positioning

  • Clopilet: For secondary prevention across the atherothrombotic spectrum
  • Clopilet A: For ideal dual anti-platelet protection from atherothrombotic spectrum
