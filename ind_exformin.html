Exformin ER

Indication

It is indicated as an adjunct to diet and exercise to improve glycemic control in adults with type 2 diabetes mellitus.

Dosage and Administration

The recommended starting dose of Exformin ER is 500 mg orally once daily with the evening meal. Increase dose in increments of 500 mg weekly, up to a maximum of 2000 mg once daily with the evening meal.

Positioning

Newly diagnosed type 2 diabetes patients.
