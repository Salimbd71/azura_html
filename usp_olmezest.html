USP- Olmezest

New Highlighting Points/ USPs:

  • Significant and consistent 24 h BP reduction
  • Faster onset of action – early lowering of BP within 2 weeks
  • Simple dosing increases patient compliance
  • Low dose combinations more effective than high dose monotherapy
  • CCB synergistically enhances the inhibitory action of Olmesartan on brain ischemia

Old Highlighting Points/ USPs:

  • Reduces blood pressure within 1-2 weeks; binding affinity to AT1 receptor is 12500 fold.
  • Exhibits powerful BP reduction even during last 4 hours of 24-hour dosing period.
  • Reduces wall to lumen ratio up to 25% (VIOS Study) - provides cardiovascular protection beyond BP control.
  • Maintains powerful BP reduction for 24 hrs due to highest RASS blockade (2009 Journal).
  • Reduces SBP & DBP significantly more than Losartan, Valsartan, Irbesartan (OPARIL Trial).
  • 23% risk reduction of microalbuminuria onset in type-2 diabetes (ROADMAP Trial).
  • Reduces vascular inflammation, lowers risk of CV events (EUTOPIA Study).
  • Reduces atherosclerotic plaque volume beyond BP control (MORE Study).
  • Bioavailability not reduced by food, unlike Losartan.
  • More patients achieved goal BP than Losartan or Valsartan.
  • AZTEC Study confirms Olmezest AM 40 ensures maximum BP reduction.
